Who we are?

Your committed partner

Concept 2 Product (C2P) , founded in 2003, is a product design, development, and manufacturing company based in Hyderabad, India. Our competence lies in providing one-stop, end-to-end solutions specializing in complex and high precision injection molding.

We apply our engineering and design prowess to handle prototype and mold design, mold fabrication, press tools, hand molds, and precision machining of plastic, rubber, and aluminium components. We pride ourselves on our high workmanship, timely delivery, competitive prices, and exceeding client expectations.

Whatever you molding needs, we believe in working closely with you at every step, from concept to production, to together craft excellent products.

We are here to listen to your ideas and concepts with the attention it deserves. We then apply our high design standards and engineering know-how to refine and transform your ideas into a superior design that exceeds your expectations while staying within budget.

PRODUCT DESIGN

RAPID PROTOTYPING (RPT)

Managing speed and cost in guiding a new product from concept to market is the challenge of today's manufacturing reality. Rapid prototyping - creating a scale model from a 3D CAD design - gives you a proof of concept which can then be refined at little cost in time and resources.

RAPID PROTOTYPING (RPT)
